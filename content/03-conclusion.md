## Conclusion

With publishpipe, you can:

- Write content in markdown with familiar tooling
- Organize long documents into manageable chapter files
- Preview changes instantly with the dev server
- Export to PDF when ready

> Chapters are concatenated in the order specified in `publishpipe.config.ts`, with horizontal rules between them.

### Next Steps

- Customize the template to match your brand
- Add more chapters as your document grows
- Use `publishpipe build` to generate the final PDF
